# Prompts para sprite sheets del pingüino chef por tarea

## 1. Pedido / WhatsApp
Create a pixel-art sprite sheet of a cute kawaii penguin chef character using a smartphone to receive an order.
The sprite sheet must be exactly 256 pixels wide by 64 pixels tall, arranged as 4 frames horizontally (each frame is 64x64 pixels).
CHARACTER DESIGN:
- Chubby adorable penguin with round white belly and dark blue/black back
- Tall white chef hat with small brim
- Magenta (#a81452) apron with small pocket
- Big expressive eyes, tiny pink blush marks, small orange beak and orange feet
- Holding a small smartphone in one flipper, reading/responding to an order
- Simple clean pixel art with thick outlines
4 FRAMES:
Frame 1: Looking at phone, neutral happy pose
Frame 2: Phone lifted slightly, one eye winking happily
Frame 3: Both eyes open, excited expression, tiny message bubble near phone
Frame 4: Content smile, phone lowered slightly, minimal sparkle or none
TECHNICAL SPECS:
- 8-bit retro pixel art, 256x64 total, 4 frames of 64x64
- Fully transparent background
- Limited flat palette, no gradients, no anti-aliasing
- Crisp pixel edges, optimized PNG
MOOD: Cute, friendly, artisanal bakery vibe.
